Abstract Banners - Large

Large Abstract Banners are closable, transparent overlays that extend over app content and generate 2-5x the earnings of traditional banner ads. (once we have metrics on how large vs small banners perform we can add 3x, 10x etc in here) While viewing the ad, the user can still access app content and can close the ads with an easy to locate close button. Airpush developers have complete control over how frequently these larger ads appear during an app session.

      - 2x - 5x traditional banner earnings.
      - Gorgeous, closable, animated ads.
      - Deep publisher controls.
      - 100% high quality ads.
      - Free weekly payments.
      - iOS and Mobile Web coming soon!

Ad Dimensions: 320x250 pixels
Ad Placement: Bottom of screen
Close Option: Yes

Abstract Banners - Video

Video Abstract Banners are completely new HTML5 video ads that load seamlessly and do not use the device’s media player. This means that they will not slow down a mobile device like traditional video ads can and generate 2-5x the earnings of traditional placements.

Banner Ads - Traditional



Traditional Banner Ads are static, motionless ads that display in a dedicated area at the bottom of an app session. Although lower in individual earnings, Traditional Banner Ads can be combined with Airpush’s advanced ad types such as Abstract Banners to result in huge overall earnings and a great user experience.

Ad Dimensions: 320x50 pixels / 728x90 pixels
Ad Placement: Bottom of screen
Close Option: No
